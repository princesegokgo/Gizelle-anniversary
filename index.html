A Letter for Gizelle

My dearest Gizelle,

I won't pretend our story has been simple. We've known what it feels like to lose each other, to walk separate paths, and to carry the weight of things that once hurt us. But somehow, here we are again… choosing each other, and that choice means more to me than anything easy ever could.

Gizelle, these past thirty days have been something I can't quite put into perfect words , but I'll try lol

You've brought a kind of warmth into my life that I didn't even realize I had missed so deeply. It's in the way you laugh, the way you speak, the little moments when you don't even try to be special but somehow are. Being with you again doesn't feel like starting over… it feels like finding something that was always meant to be, just waiting for the right time.

These days with you have reminded me how beautiful simple things can be. Talking with you, spending time together, even silence feels different like it actually means something. Like it's ours.

This isn't just about a month. It's about a second chance that I don't take lightly. It's about rebuilding something stronger, more honest, more intentional.

And if this is only the beginning… then I'm ready to turn every page with you, slowly, carefully, making sure this story is one we're both proud of.

I'm here, Gizelle. Not just for now but for what we're building.

Happy one month to us 🌹

Forever yours 🌹
